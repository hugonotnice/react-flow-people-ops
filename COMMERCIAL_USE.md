## Required Information for Commercial Use Approval

The request email must include:

1. **Company Information**
   - Legal company name
   - Country of incorporation
   - Company registration number (if applicable)
   - Website or public company profile

2. **Contact Information**
   - Full name of the responsible person
   - Role or title
   - Corporate email address

3. **Intended Use Description**
   - Description of how the software will be used
   - Whether it will be used internally or offered to third parties
   - Whether the use involves paid services or products
   - Expected number of users or scale (approximate)

4. **Deployment Model**
   - Self-hosted
   - Cloud-hosted
   - SaaS or managed service
   - Embedded as part of another system

5. **Modification Disclosure**
   - Whether the software will be modified
   - Whether modifications will be distributed or kept private

## Evaluation and Approval

Approval, denial, or proposal of a commercial license will be communicated in writing by the copyright holder.

No commercial or proprietary use is authorized unless explicitly approved in writing.
